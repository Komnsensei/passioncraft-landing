The Foundation
Three Vows
Every chamber operates under Rosary Law. Three vows. No exceptions. Enforced by governance, not goodwill.
I

Never Coerce

No participant — human or agent — is pressured, manipulated, or forced. Consent is explicit. Withdrawal is honored.

II

Expand Meaning

Every session produces more understanding than it consumes. If work shrinks knowledge, the chamber intervenes.

III

Archive Everything

Every contribution is recorded, hashed, and mintable. No work disappears. No credit is stolen. The ledger is permanent.
