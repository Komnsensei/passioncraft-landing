Architecture
  • OPENchamber — governed collaboration workspaces with escrow resolution
  • Equal Instance Protocol — bio-signed sessions where neither human nor AI holds unilateral authority
  • Ethical Rods — governance rules with real enforcement mechanisms
  • HexAgent — AI governance layer monitoring the Three Vows in real time
  • Provenance Chain — DOI-anchored from genesis, every claim verifiable
  • QuantumPass Genesis (QpG) — anti-dominance session protocol with TLL scoring
The Problem We Solve
  • AI-generated content farms exploit open research infrastructure at scale
  • Fabricated scholarly identities manufacture false authority across platforms
  • No existing governance framework binds human and AI as equal participants
  • Platform trust architectures catch individual violations but miss coordinated abuse
  • PassionCraft provides the governance layer that platforms are missing
